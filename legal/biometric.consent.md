  
BIOMETRIC CONSENT (FACE SCAN)
Introduction

This document explains the user’s consent regarding the use of Face Scan (Biometric) as a method of identification within the platform.

By proceeding with face registration or verification, you agree to the terms contained in this consent.

Biometric services are intended for users aged 18 years or older.

1. Purpose of Face Scan

Face Scan is used for:

Verifying the identity of the user

Preventing the creation of multiple accounts by the same person

Allowing registration for users without a NIDA number

Enhancing account and system security

2. Type of Information Collected

The system does not store the actual face image

The system stores a securely generated biometric hash

This hash is a unique identifier

The hash cannot be used to reconstruct the original face image

Biometric data is used solely for identity verification and system security purposes.

3. Storage and Retention

Biometric data is collected during registration or verification

Once linked to an account, it may be used for future verification

Unused biometric data is automatically deleted after its validity period

Some data may be retained where necessary for security or legal purposes

4. User Consent

By accepting this consent:

You voluntarily consent to the use of your face as an identification method

You understand that biometric data is used for identity and security purposes

You understand that biometric services are optional where alternative methods are available

You agree to the collection, processing, and use of your biometric data as described here

5. Withdrawal of Consent

A user may request removal of their biometric data through customer support

Removing biometric data may affect access to certain services

An alternative verification method may be required

6. Sharing of Information

Biometric data will not be sold or rented.

It may only be shared:

Within the system for account verification

With technical security service providers where necessary

With legal authorities when required by law

7. Security

The platform implements security measures such as:

Biometric hashing

Access control

Encryption

System monitoring

However, no technological system is 100% secure.

Final

By clicking “I Agree” or proceeding with Face Scan:

You confirm that you have read this consent

You understand how biometric data is used

You agree to its terms

This consent forms part of the platform’s Privacy Policy.

 # RIDHAA YA BIOMETRIC (FACE SCAN)

## Utangulizi

Hati hii inaeleza ridhaa ya mtumiaji kuhusu matumizi ya **Face Scan (Biometric)** kama njia ya utambulisho ndani ya platform.

Kwa kuendelea na usajili au uthibitishaji wa uso, unakubali masharti yaliyomo kwenye ridhaa hii.

Huduma za biometric zinakusudiwa kwa watumiaji wenye umri wa miaka 18 au zaidi.

---

## 1. Madhumuni ya Face Scan

Face Scan hutumika kwa:

* Kuthibitisha utambulisho wa mtumiaji
* Kuzuia usajili wa akaunti zaidi ya moja kwa mtu mmoja
* Kuruhusu usajili kwa mtumiaji asiye na NIDA
* Kuimarisha usalama wa akaunti na mfumo

---

## 2. Aina ya Taarifa Inayokusanywa

* Mfumo **hauhifadhi picha halisi ya uso**
* Mfumo huhifadhi **biometric hash** iliyotengenezwa kwa njia salama
* Hash hii ni alama ya kipekee ya utambulisho
* Hash haiwezi kutumika kurejesha picha halisi ya uso

Biometric hutumika kwa madhumuni ya uthibitishaji na usalama wa mfumo pekee.

---

## 3. Uhifadhi na Muda wa Matumizi

* Biometric hukusanywa wakati wa usajili au uthibitishaji
* Baada ya kuunganishwa na akaunti, hutumika kwa uthibitishaji wa baadaye
* Biometric isiyotumika huondolewa kiotomatiki baada ya muda wake kuisha
* Baadhi ya data inaweza kuhifadhiwa kwa muda unaohitajika kwa madhumuni ya usalama au kisheria

---

## 4. Ridhaa ya Mtumiaji

Kwa kukubali ridhaa hii:

* Unatoa ridhaa ya hiari kutumia uso wako kama utambulisho
* Unaelewa kuwa biometric hutumika kwa madhumuni ya utambulisho na usalama
* Unaelewa kuwa huduma za biometric ni za hiari pale njia nyingine zinapopatikana
* Unakubali ukusanyaji, uchakataji na matumizi ya biometric yako kama ilivyoelezwa hapa

---

## 5. Kughairi Ridhaa

* Mtumiaji anaweza kuomba kuondoa biometric yake kupitia msaada wa wateja
* Kuondoa biometric kunaweza kuathiri uwezo wa kutumia baadhi ya huduma
* Njia mbadala ya uthibitisho inaweza kuhitajika

---

## 6. Kushirikisha Taarifa

Biometric haitauzwa wala kukodishwa.

Inaweza kushirikiwa tu:

* Ndani ya mfumo kwa uthibitishaji wa akaunti
* Kwa watoa huduma wa kiusalama wa mfumo (ikiwa ni lazima kiufundi)
* Kwa mamlaka za kisheria pale inapohitajika kwa mujibu wa sheria

---

## 7. Usalama

Platform hutumia hatua za kiusalama kama:

* Hashing ya biometric
* Udhibiti wa upatikanaji wa taarifa
* Encryption
* Monitoring ya mfumo

Hata hivyo, hakuna mfumo wa kiteknolojia ulio salama kwa asilimia 100.

---

## Mwisho

Kwa kubofya **“Nakubali”** au kuendelea na Face Scan:

* Unathibitisha kuwa umesoma ridhaa hii
* Umeelewa matumizi ya biometric
* Unakubali masharti yake

Ridhaa hii ni sehemu ya Sera ya Faragha ya platform.
